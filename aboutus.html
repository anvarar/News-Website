About us

Newstodayne.com is the digital platform of the English Eveninger News Today that reports on politics, social, sports, entertainment, lifestyle stories across the globe, with a specific focus on Tamilnadu.

The media house was founded by doyen of journalism T R Ramaswamy in 1982 and is now ably led by his son Editor T R Jawahar, who is a chartered accountant by profession but journalist by passion.

Our content includes news reporting, news analysis and opinions. Headquartered in Chennai, News Today takes an unbiased views on news and happenings. Jawahar has made a name for himself writing a series of articles on politics and socio-happenings through his Point Blank column. They are popular and widely read.
