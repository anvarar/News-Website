Chandrayaan-3 LIVE Updates: Chandrayaan-3 inches closer to moon after successful orbit reduction manoeuvre

The Chandrayaan-3 spacecraft on Sunday successfully entered the lunar orbit. If the rest of the current mission goes to plan, the mission will safely touch down near the Moon's little-explored south pole between August 23 and 24. Since its launch on July 14, ISRO has been lifting the Chandrayaan-3 spacecraft into orbits farther and farther away from Earth. Chandrayaan-3 LIVE Updates: Next orbit reduction manoeuvre to take place on August 9 Chandrayaan-3 LIVE updates: After the successful upgradation of Chandrayaan-3 orbit on Sunday night, next orbit reduction manoeuvre will be performed on August 9, ISRO said in a tweet. ISRO successfully performs orbit reduction manoeuvre, Chandrayaan-3 closer to moon Chandrayaan 3 LIVE Updates: ISRO conducted the orbit reduction manoeuvre of Chandrayaan-3 on Sunday night. After this, Chandrayaan-3 has inched closer to the moon.
